Brendon Urie

Brendon Boyd Urie is an American singer, songwriter and artist most well know for his lead role in the band Panic! At The Disco. His well known song I write sins not tragedies has a whopping 318 million views!

Urie was born on the 12th of April 1987 in St. George, Utah. His family moved to Las Vegas, Nevada, when he was 2 years old and so he considers Vegas his home. He is of about one quarter Polynesian descent from Hawaii, through his mother's side. He was raised a strict Mormon but left those beliefs when he was a teenager and instead decided to pursue his music career.

Panic! At The Disco have released 6 albums while Brendon has been their lead singer.

These are:

  • A Fever You Can't Sweat Out
  • Pretty. Odd.
  • Vices & Virtues
  • Too Weird To Live, Too Rare To Die!
  • Death of a Bachelor
  • Pray for the Wicked
